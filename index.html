Ethibe

Truly community based Ethical financial app.

Example: The church of England Financial Community or Elon Musk’s Fans Financial community

Why?

Community is based on common interest and aspirations. However, there is no financial business models that is truly based on and created for communities common financial interest and aspirations.

What?

A Financial environment. Created by your community. Based on the your community values. For you and members of your community.

How?

We work with different communities to create financial services and financial well being via Ethibe.com app which is based on these communities values and ethics for the members of these communities.

Who?

Members of Religious communities, Universities students, Fans of stars/celebrities, charity organisation members and members of other profit or non profit community based organisations.

Vision & Mission

Rebuilding corporative and community banking model from ground zero.

Truly cooperative, truly community as a result truly ethical financial system.

Ethibe.com is an Ethical Financial App Truly Based on your Community Ethics and Values.

features

Save, spend and invest based on your community values and ethics.

Join a savings club (Ajo Kiko) with member of your community.

Find and adopt Suitable financial instruments such as life insurance that is in accordance with your community ethics and values.

Learn and put into practice, directly in our app, financial habits for your financial well-being that are based on your community teachings.

Lend money to or borrow money from your community members based on your community set rules and regulations.
